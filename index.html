About Us

Connecting capital with high-impact opportunities

VC Partners is a Vietnam-based alternative asset investment/development platform and advisory firm committed to connecting capital with high-impact opportunities. We design tailored solutions that generate sustainable value for investors and partners alike. Our expertise spans real estate, hospitality, industrial & logistics, healthcare, education, and infrastructure—enabling us to support transformative projects that contribute to Vietnam's long-term growth.

Competitive Advantages

Proven Track Record

Our team has consistently delivered strong results in Vietnam real estate, with past projects achieving 20–35% IRRs and 2–3x equity multiples.

Deep Local Knowledge & Relationships

Through longstanding partnerships with key government and business stakeholders, we offer unmatched insight into domestic demand drivers and regulatory frameworks.

High Quality Deal Pipeline

Our network and track record provide access to exclusive, off-market opportunities. We are positioned to source, execute, and manage some of the most compelling projects in Vietnam.

Client-Centric Strategy

By prioritizing clients' success, we reduce investment risk and strengthen long-term partnerships. Our team provides post-investment/development support, ensuring that our clients are well-positioned for sustainable growth in Vietnam.

Services

Fund Investment & Management

Real Estate Development

End-to-end project development and execution, including investment structuring, legal entitlements, project management, and strategic partnerships, to unlock long-term value in Vietnam's property sector.

M&A Advisory

Unlocking value through strategic mergers, acquisitions, and divestments, designed to maximize shareholder outcomes.

Our Team

VC Partners was founded by Mr. Truong The Kiet, CFA, CAIA, a Vietnamese American investment professional with nearly three decades of experience across the U.S. and Asia-Pacific. He is the Founder of VC Partners, Five Elements Development and Vietbridge Capital.

Mr. Kiet previously served as Director at Indochina Capital—Vietnam's first real estate fund—and as Analyst at Morgan Stanley. He has a proven track record in capital advisory, M&A, and real estate development. Holding degrees from Columbia University and Harvard University, and as a CFA and CAIA charterholder, he combines global expertise with deep local insight to lead VC Partners.

ESG

Environmental

  • We are committed to achieving EDGE certification across all buildings and construction projects in our portfolio—driving measurable reductions of 20% in water usage, energy consumption, and embodied energy in materials.
  • We will deploy rooftop solar systems on our cold storage, logistics and e-commerce facilities, generating clean solar power to support Vietnam's transition to renewable energy.

Social

Food Security

  • We will partner with FoodBank Vietnam to improve access to nutritious meals for vulnerable communities, particularly in areas where our projects operate.

Schools

  • Through partnerships with our contractors, we will fund the construction of 20 schools in underserved rural areas and provide scholarships for students, especially children of workers engaged in our projects.

Governance

  • Our platform operates under a robust governance framework that safeguards investment processes, internal practices, and employee conduct through strict controls and oversight.
  • We adhere to third-party audits to ensure our financials are accurate, transparent, and comprehensive.
  • We maintain a fair and competitive compensation structure, designed to attract and retain top talent while upholding accountability and integrity across all operations.
